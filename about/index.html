About Keifer's

    Keifer's Cafe was started by Jordan "Keifer" Higham in 2015. As a young boy, Keifer and his family would visit Maui, where he always had a dream of opening up a luxury seafood restaurant right off of the coast. Keifer's slogan has always been this: "The views here at Keifer's are spectacular, but the food is even better. We here at Keifer's Cafe strive daily to provide our guests with a dining experience that is umatched. Every single one of our chefs has been rated 5 out of 5 stars by the Chefs Association of America. Our servers our taught to treat everyone like family and help each guest get the very most of their experience. Our number one goal at Keifer's is to have you leave our restaurant happier than when you arrived.
