About Keifers Cafe

    Keifers Cafe was started by Jordan C. Higham also know as "Keifer" in 2015. As a young boy Keifer and his family would visit Maui, and Keifer always had a dream of opening up a luxury seafood restaurant right off of the coast, Keifers motto was always this, the views here at Keifers are spectacular but the food is even better. We here at Keifers strive daily to provide our guests with a dining experience that is umatched. Every single one of our Chefs has been rated 5 out of 5 stars by the Chefs Association of America. Our servers our taught to treat everyone like family and help each guest get the very most of their experience. Our number one goal at Keifers, is that you will leave our restaurant happier then when you arrived.
